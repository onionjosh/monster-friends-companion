These abilities can be used by **any monster** in the game.

## Last Life — Cost 3

When a monster is reduced to **0 Health Points**, reduce them to 1 Health Point instead. **They can't take any more damage until the start of the next turn.**

Give that monster a Last Life token. Monsters with a Last Life token **cannot be targeted by this ability.**

**Every time this ability is used, increase the cost by +1 energy until the end of the game** (this does not affect your opponent's cost).

## Pile Drive — Cost 1

If a monster lands on an enemy monster below them as a result of [[rule:falling|falling]], they can make a Pile Drive attack. **Make a Basic Attack against that monster with a +2 Bonus for every 2" they fell.** Then, the monster who made the attack takes their Fall Damage.

## Bonus Action — Cost 3

At the start of your turn, give any monster you control **1 Action Token**.

## Bolster — Cost 1+

Before making an Attack Dice roll, you can decide to Bolster the roll. **Add +1 to your next Attack Dice roll for every energy you spend.**

## Block — Cost 1+

Before your opponent makes an Attack Dice roll (or after they choose to Bolster an attack), you can decide to Block. **Add +1 to your next Defense Dice for every energy you spend.**

## Throw an Obstacle — Cost 1 Energy + 1 Action Token

This ability can only be used by **Medium and Large** monsters who are modeled with a limb that could reasonably throw a small object. Select a [[kw:small-obstacle|Small Obstacle]] within 1" of the monster who used this ability. **Move it up to 6" in a straight line,** then make the following Basic Attack against a single enemy monster within 1" of where it lands:

> **Thrown Obstacle (Ranged)** — Range 6", Swings 1, Attack Dice d6, Bonus +2, Crit Bonus +2
