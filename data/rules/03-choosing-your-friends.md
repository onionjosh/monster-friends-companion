**Before the game**, you and your opponent need to decide how many [[kw:party-points|Party Points]] you want to play. That number determines the maximum number of Party Points worth of monsters you can each bring to the game.

**For new players**, we recommend playing a **50 Party Point** game. Any number lower than this is also fine if you don't have enough monster friends to play a full 50 point game, but want to try the core rules.

**For experienced players**, once you've played a few games, we recommend trying **51–100 Party Points** (we find **75** to be our current favorite spot).

## Party Point COST

**Every monster** has a **Party Point Cost** listed on their character card. That number is how many Party Points that monster costs in order to be **included in your party**.

**Each player** can take up to as many Party Points worth of monsters as the game size they've chosen allows. **There is no maximum number** of models a player can take.
