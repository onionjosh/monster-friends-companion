title: Activating a Monster
order: 7
---
There are **6** different kinds of Actions a monster can perform.

Every monster can perform up to as many Actions per Activation as they have Action Tokens. Every Action costs 1 Action Token, with the exception of Charge *(and Special Abilities, which usually vary between 0 to 2)*.

- **Move:** The monster moves using the Movement speed described on their Monster Card (see [[rule:moving|Moving Monsters]]).
- **Basic Attack:** The monster can perform any basic attack listed on their Monster Card.
- **[[kw:charge|Charge]] (Costs 2):** A monster can only be selected to charge if there are no enemy monsters within 1" of them. Increase their Movement stat by 2x until the end of the Charge action. Then, they can perform a free Move action. They must end this move within 1" of an enemy. Then, they can perform a free Basic Attack action with a +2 Bonus Modifier.
- **[[kw:guard|Guard]]:** The monster receives a +2 bonus to Defense Dice rolls until the next time they activate *(or until the end of the Round, whichever comes first)*.
- **[[kw:rest|Rest]]:** Gain +2 [[kw:energy|Energy]] *(each monster can only rest once per Round)*.
- **Special Ability:** The monster can use any of the special abilities on their monster card.

> **PARTY TIPS**
> - There are some Special Abilities that can't be used during a monster's own activation, and are instead used at a time specified in that Special Ability's description.
> - Monsters can sometimes receive **Action Tokens and HP** through things like Special Abilities. There is no limit to how many Action Tokens a monster can have, but a monster cannot exceed their maximum HP characteristic through healing.
