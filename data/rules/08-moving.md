title: Moving Monsters

---
**Every time** a monster does a move action, it can move up to as many inches as the **Movement** stat listed on its character card. You can rotate your monster to change the direction they're facing as many times as you wish while moving them for free.

## Moving through models

Monsters may move over friendly monsters, but cannot end their move on top of them. Monsters cannot move through enemy Monsters (their base must be able to fit through any gaps between enemies in order to complete the move action). Unlike some other wargames, you are allowed to move within 1" of enemy monsters, you just cannot move through their actual base.

## Surrounded!

Because monsters can't move over enemy monsters, there will be instances where a monster might become **Surrounded**, and not legally be able to move. If at the start of their move, your monster would have to move over an enemy model to make a legal move and wants to do so, **every enemy monster within 1" of them is allowed to make a free Basic Attack.**

## Overhanging Monster Limbs

**Many monsters are weirdly shaped,** and have big awkward limbs that might prevent them from fitting somewhere you want them to go. For all rules purposes, only the base of a monster is used to determine where that monster can move and whether or not it can fit somewhere.
