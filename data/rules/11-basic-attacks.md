There are two different kinds of Basic Attacks: **Melee Attacks** and **Ranged Attacks.** If a monster has multiple Basic Attacks listed on their Monster Card, they choose one to use per activation.

## Melee Attacks

To make a Melee Attack, a monster you control must be within 1" horizontally and 2" their height vertically of a monster you want to attack. You are not allowed to attack your own monsters with a Basic Attack.

Many monsters will have multiple Basic Attacks listed on their Monster Card. Whenever you attack, if a monster has more than one Basic Attack listed on their Monster Card, you can only choose to do 1 of them per action performed.

**Then, choose the Basic Attack you want to use from those listed on your monster's profile.**

## Attack Dice and Defense Dice

Roll a number of dice equal to the **Swings** stat specified in your Basic Attack's description. The type of dice a monster rolls varies depending on the Basic Attack. Then, add all bonuses and modifiers to each of the dice rolled.

At the same time, your opponent rolls the **Defense Dice** listed on their monster card, and adds all of their bonuses and modifiers. Just like Basic Attacks, the type of dice varies depending on the monster who is defending.

Subtract the total of the **Defense Dice** roll from the total of the **Attack Dice** roll. Their monster takes damage equal to the difference. If the result is less than 0, they don't take any damage.

## Defense Example

As an example, here is Bucket Troll's Defense Dice stat: **Defense Dice d8, Bonus +0, Crit Bonus +2.**

Bucket Troll's Defense Dice is a d8. Whenever he makes a roll, he rolls a d8. His bonus is +0, so whatever the dice lands on is the result of the roll. If he rolls an 8 **(defense dice can Crit just like attack dice do)**, he adds +2 to the roll.
