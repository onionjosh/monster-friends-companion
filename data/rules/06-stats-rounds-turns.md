## MONSTER STATS

Every Monster Card lists the following stats:

- **Party Points (PP)** — what the monster costs to include in your party
- **Defense (D)** — the die, Bonus, and Crit Bonus rolled when defending
- **Action Tokens (AcT)** — how many actions the monster gets each Round
- **Health Points (HP)** — how much damage the monster can take
- **Movement (M)** — how many inches the monster moves per Move action
- **Emotional Instability (EmI)** — the 2d6 target for Emotional Instability tests

## Rounds

**At the start of each Round,** every monster receives a number of [[kw:action-tokens|Action Tokens]] equal to their Action Token stat (most monsters have 3). Action Tokens are spent on Actions, which allow monsters to play the game.

When **all** monsters have 0 Action Tokens, the round is over, and a new round begins. Both players receive **+10 [[kw:energy|Energy]]** (to a maximum of 10) at the start of each Round.

## Turns

**At the start of their turn,** the player whose turn it currently is must choose any single monster with an Action Token that they want to activate (unless none of their models have Action Tokens).

**That monster must spend at least 1 Action Token.** After they have finished activating that monster (or if none of their models have Action Tokens), it becomes their opponent's turn.
