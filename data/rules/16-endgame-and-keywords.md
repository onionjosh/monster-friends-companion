## Passing the Turn

After your monster has **finished their activation**, your turn ends and it becomes your **opponent's turn.**

## Game Length

Game Length will vary depending on the game mode or scenario being played, but generally games go until a specific victory condition has been met. For your first game, we recommend the scenario **Fighting is Fun**.

More scenarios will be added to the Monster Friends Scenario Pack when we finish writing it.

## Fighting is Fun!

**How To Win:** If your opponent has 0 monsters left on the field, you win.

## Base Sizes

Models in Monster Friends have the following base sizes. While you are free to base your Monster Friends on whatever base size you want, these are the official sizes!

- Small Monsters — **28mm**
- Medium Monsters — **40mm**
- Large Monsters — **60mm**

## Ability Key Words

- **[[kw:bonded|Bonded]]** — paired monsters that fight (and panic) together. Tap for the full rules.
- **[[kw:crit-bonus|Crit Bonus]]** — bonus damage on Critical Hits.
- **[[kw:duelist|Duelist]]** — re-roll Attack Dice from this Basic Attack.
- **[[kw:executioner|Executioner]]** — kills made by this attack can't be saved by Last Life.
- **[[kw:follow-along|Follow Along]]** — tags along when friends move nearby.
- **[[kw:glide-hop|Glide / Hop]]** — ignores terrain and enemies while moving; no fall damage.
- **[[kw:hard-to-kill|Hard To Kill]]** — re-roll this monster's Defense Dice.
- **[[kw:redirect|Redirect]]** — intercept Basic Attacks aimed at friends.
- **[[kw:reposition|Reposition]]** — free Move after the attack/ability resolves.
