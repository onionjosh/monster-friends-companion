As an example, here is the Basic Attack of a *Gnorc Pillager*:

> **Gnorc Bonk (Melee)** — Swings: 2, Attack Dice: d6, Bonus: +1, Crit Bonus: +2

## What Happens?

"Gnorc Bonk" is the name of the **Basic Attack.** It is a **Melee** attack.

Whenever a Gnorc Pillager makes this Basic Attack, they roll **2** Attack Dice (each Attack Dice is a d6). After the Gnorc has rolled their Attack Dice, add **+1** to each of them.

**Then, subtract the result of the defending monster's Defense Dice from each of the Attack Dice.**

Let's say the attacking Gnorc rolls a 3 and a 6, and has no other bonuses or modifiers. Let's say the defending monster has a Defense Dice of d6 + 0 and rolls a 4.

The 3 would deal **0** damage to that monster, since 3 + 1 − 4 is 0.

The 6 however would score a [[kw:critical-hit|Critical Hit]], for a total of 6 + 1 + 2 = **9**. The defending monster would then take 9 − 4 damage, for a total of **5** damage.

> Gnorcs are territorial little beasts who seldom travel alone. If you see one be wary of more lurking in the hills!
