**Monster Friends** is designed to be played with **2 players**, so you will need a friend or random game store person that you force to play with you.

> We are working on rules for 3 and 4 player games. Please feel free to also make up your own rules to add to the game. We would like this game to be a modular sandbox for others to explore and invent content for.

## You'll WANT the FOLLOWING

- A roughly 2 feet by 2 feet playing area
- A handful of six sided dice
- A handful of small tokens to keep track of various abilities
- A set of polyhedral d4+ dice
- A small amount of fantasy or Monster Friends inspired terrain
