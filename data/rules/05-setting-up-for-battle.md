**Future scenarios** will have a variety of different rules for how and where to set up your monsters, but to get started, we recommend players grabbing a small handful of terrain, placing it wherever you and your opponent decide **would be most fun**, and using the standard deployment layout: each player deploys wholly within **4"** of their own table edge, on opposite sides of the board.

**The player whose birthday** is closest to today's date is **Player 1**. The other player is Player 2. If both players have the same birthday, **THIS GAME CANNOT BE PLAYED**.

## Picking Order

Starting with Player 1, players alternate placing monsters one by one anywhere that is wholly within **4"** of their respective table edges. This process is repeated until both players have placed all of the monsters in their party.

When both players are done, they roll off to see who goes first. The winner of the roll off decides who goes first. **Player 1 receives +1 to the roll.**

At the start of every subsequent round, **the player who ran out of action tokens first receives +1 to the roll**.
