---
When a Monster falls, move them from their current position to the first surface they can fit over half their base on directly beneath where they fell from.

> **PARTY TIP** — When a Monster falls, move them from their current position to anywhere within 2" horizontally of the point from which they fell.

## Fall Damage

**For every full 1" taller than their height a Monster falls, roll a d6:**

- **For every 1,** they take 4 damage.
- **For every 2-3,** they take 2 damage.
- **For every 4-6,** they have managed to fall semi safely, and take no damage.

## FOOL OF ICARUS

Monsters modeled on tall scenic bases **EXPLICITLY FOR COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE** take an additional automatic 3 damage per 1" fallen.

## Choosing to Fall

**If a Monster chooses to fall** by jumping off a ledge, they don't have to pay for the vertical distance traveled to the bottom of the location they're falling from.

If they want to, a monster can choose to fall by jumping off the side of a piece of terrain instead of climbing down.

If a monster is pushed off the edge of a piece of vertical terrain by an ability, they fall.
