**Monster Friends: Battle For New Florida** is a miniature skirmish game which aims to make every playable monster mechanically novel and unique, and do so with a very accessible learning curve.

## WHY is this game ??

We feel there is a large emphasis on 'balance' within many miniature games, and want to instead pursue a game where every monster feels broken and flavorful — in a way that is hopefully **F U N**.

We want to create a game where no model feels generic, and for every monster to feel fun and rewarding to play.

## WHO are you?

You are a **Monster Friend Party Planner** who hails from **New Florida**. New Florida is the place where all Monster Friends live and do Battle. Party Planners train Monster Friends and show them how to fight **Good**.

> Monster Friends actively seek out Party Planners, and generally enjoy battling with each other (it is all for fun). You are helping them by playing this game. You are also helping us by playing this game.

## HOW DO I WIN?

Win conditions will vary depending on the scenario. For your first game, we recommend playing the scenario [[rule:endgame-and-keywords|Fighting is Fun]].

## How Long is a Game?

Games *typically* last from **30 to 45 minutes** but can be shorter or longer depending on a player's [[kw:party-points|Party Points]].
